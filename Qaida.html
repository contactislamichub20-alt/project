About This Course

Noorani Qaida is the essential beginner’s course for learning the Arabic alphabet and foundational Quranic reading skills. This course guides learners step-by-step through pronunciation, letter recognition, and basic tajweed rules to build a strong base for Quran recitation. Suitable for all ages, Noorani Qaida prepares students to confidently progress towards reading the Quran fluently and accurately.

Starting at $50 Per Month

  • Levels = 1 Levels
  • Duration = 3 months
  • Subject = Arabic Language
  • Language = English / Arabic

Learning Objectives

What You Will Learn

  • Recognition and pronunciation of individual Arabic letters.
  • How to connect letters to form words.
  • Basic rules of reading Arabic from right to left.
  • A strong foundation for reading the Holy Quran correctly.

This Course is Ideal For

  • Beginners of all ages starting their Quran reading journey.
  • Children and adults learning to read Arabic for the first time.
  • Anyone seeking a structured and easy-to-follow method.
  • Those looking for the essential first step before learning Tajweed.
