Islamic Studies – Full Track is a comprehensive course designed to introduce children to the core teachings of Islam in an engaging and age-appropriate way. Through interactive lessons, quizzes, and activities, kids develop a deep love and understanding of their faith.

Starting at $55 Per Month

  • Subject = Islamic Studies

Learning Objectives

What You Will Learn

  • Foundational knowledge of Tawhid (Oneness of Allah).
  • Stories of the Prophets and their teachings.
  • Basic principles of Islamic worship and practices.
  • Understanding Islamic manners and ethics.

This Course is Ideal For
